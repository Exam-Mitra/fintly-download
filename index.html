What you get

  • ● Create a companion with a name, relationship, and personality that feels like "them"
  • ● Warm, natural conversations that remember the flow of your chat
  • ● Tap the speaker icon to hear your companion's replies read aloud
  • ● A private mood journal to check in with yourself each day
  • ● Your conversations stay on your device — no public profile, no social feed

How to install

  1. Tap the Download APK button above.
  2. Once downloaded, open the file from your notification bar or Downloads folder.
  3. If prompted, tap Settings → allow "Install unknown apps" for your browser, then go back and tap Install.
  4. Open Fintly Companion and say hi to your new companion.
